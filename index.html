Climate & Urban Planning

Climate change brings about new complexities in maintaining the wellbeing of society and the environment in cities. Natural resources, ecosystems, and infrastructure must be monitored to ensure human quality of life remains high. This app demonstrates how urban planners can use NASA Earth observation data to develop smart strategies for city growth while protecting both people and the environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the NASA data?

Our app uses real-time NASA Earth observation data with high accuracy for climate and environmental monitoring.

Can I export reports?

Yes, you can export detailed reports in PDF format for research and policy-making purposes.

Is the app free to use?

Basic features are free. Premium features for advanced analytics require a subscription.

How often is data updated?

Climate data is updated every 6 hours, while satellite imagery is refreshed daily.
